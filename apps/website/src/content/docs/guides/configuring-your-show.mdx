## Changing the first page's number

OpenMarch automatically numbers every page as an increment of 1 from the first page, taking into account subsets.
By default, the first page's name is `0`, but you can change it to any number you want.

<Steps>

1. Open the <span style={{ display: "inline-flex", gap: "0.25rem" }}><SlidersHorizontalIcon /> </span> `Workspace Settings` tab in the bottom left corner
1. Change the `Page Number Offset` to the number you want
1. Click `Save Changes`
1. The new first page number will be displayed immediately in the timeline

</Steps>

## Changing the starting measure's number

OpenMarch automatically numbers every measure as an increment of 1 from the starting measure.
By default, the starting measure number is `1`, but you can change it to any number you want.

<Steps>

1. Open the <span style={{ display: "inline-flex", gap: "0.25rem" }}><SlidersHorizontalIcon /> </span> `Workspace Settings` tab in the bottom left corner
1. Change the `Measure Number Offset` to the number you want
1. Click `Save Changes`
1. The new starting measure number will be displayed immediately in the timeline

</Steps>
