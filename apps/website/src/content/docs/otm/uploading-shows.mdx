All shows are uploaded to OTM from the desktop app and require an OpenMarch account to create and publish.

## Uploading to OTM for the first time

<Steps>

1. Create an Ensemble and Production in the [Ensemble Management Dashboard](https://app.openmarch.com/).

   See [Getting Started](/docs/otm/getting-started) for more information on what an Ensemble and Production are.

1. Open the OpenMarch Desktop app with the show you want to upload.

1. Click the <span style={{ display: "inline-flex", gap: "0.25rem" }}><DeviceMobileIcon /> </span> Phone icon in the bottom left corner.

1. Press `Sign in` to connect your account to the desktop app.

1. Click the Production you want to upload to and click `Attach File to Production`.

1. Then, click "Publish to mobile app".

1. The show will be instantly available to all performers registered to the ensemble.

</Steps>

![Uploading to OTM for the first time](/docs/otm/initial-publish-desktop.gif)

## Uploading revisions to an existing production

OpenMarch allows you to upload **Revisions** to an existing production.
This is useful for making changes to you show after you publish it initially.

<Steps>

1. Open the OpenMarch Desktop app with the show you want to upload.

1. Click the <span style={{ display: "inline-flex", gap: "0.25rem" }}><DeviceMobileIcon /> </span> Phone icon in the bottom left corner.

   You should see your Production you attached the file to previously.

1. Type a small message describing what changed in the production.

   This is not visible to users on the mobile app. It's just for your reference.

1. Press `Publish to mobile app`.

1. The updates will be instantly available to all performers registered to the ensemble.

</Steps>

![Uploading revisions to an existing production](/docs/otm/upload-revision.gif)
